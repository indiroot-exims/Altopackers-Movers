Our Services

Complete relocation solutions tailored to your needs, from Vadodara across Gujarat and beyond.

HOUSEHOLD RELOCATION

Household Relocation

Moving your home is one of life's biggest decisions. Our household relocation service ensures that every piece of furniture, every box, and every memory is transported safely and carefully from your old home to your new one. Whether it's a local move within Vadodara, across Gujarat, or to another state, our experienced team handles your belongings with the care they deserve.

We provide complete end-to-end solutions: professional packing, secure loading, GPS-tracked transport, and careful unpacking and setup at your destination. Your home is our priority.

Professional packing with premium materials
Secure, insured transport with tracking
Careful unpacking and room setup included
OFFICE RELOCATION

Office Relocation

A successful office move means zero downtime and seamless continuity. Alto's corporate relocation specialists plan, coordinate, and execute office moves with military precision. From small startups to large enterprises, we've relocated offices, warehouses, and manufacturing units across Vadodara and Gujarat.

Our approach minimizes business disruption: we handle IT equipment, furniture, important documents, and operational assets with specialized packing and transport. Your business stays on track.

Minimal downtime and business disruption
IT equipment and sensitive asset handling
Workspace setup and equipment installation
VEHICLE SHIFTING

Vehicle Shifting

Whether you're relocating with your car or two-wheeler, our vehicle shifting service ensures safe transport across Vadodara, Gujarat, and India. We use specialized carriers and trained drivers who understand the value of your vehicle. Every journey is insured, tracked, and delivered on schedule.

From sedan to SUV, bikes to commercial vehicles, our fleet and expertise cover all types of vehicle transport. Your car arrives in the same condition it left.

Specialized open or closed carriers
Full insurance coverage for your vehicle
GPS tracking and real-time updates
LOADING & UNLOADING

Loading & Unloading

Expert handling is the foundation of a successful move. Our trained loading and unloading teams understand the weight, balance, and placement of every item. From fragile antiques to heavy furniture, every item is treated with precision and respect.

This service is perfect for those who prefer to pack their own items but want professional, careful loading into the truck and unloading at the destination. Safety and efficiency guaranteed.

Trained and experienced loading teams
Careful handling of fragile and heavy items
Optimized truck space utilization
PACKING & UNPACKING

Packing & Unpacking

Quality packing is the secret to a damage-free move. Alto uses only premium packing materials—corrugated boxes, bubble wrap, foam, protective paper, and custom crating for delicate items. Each item is carefully wrapped, labeled, and organized for safe transport and easy unpacking.

Our unpacking service goes beyond just opening boxes. We carefully remove, arrange, and place every item in its designated location at your new home or office. Your new space is organized and ready from day one.

Premium packing materials and supplies
Careful labeling and inventory tracking
Organized arrangement at new location
WAREHOUSE & STORAGE

Warehouse & Storage Services

Sometimes you need time to move, renovate, or expand. Our secure warehouse and storage facility in Vadodara offers climate-controlled, insured storage for short and long-term needs. Whether you need a few weeks or several months, your belongings are safe with us.

State-of-the-art security, regular pest control, and careful handling ensure your items remain in perfect condition. Access your items anytime during business hours.

Climate-controlled secure storage facility
Full insurance coverage on stored items
Flexible short and long-term options
WOODEN PACKING

Wooden Packing

For high-value items, antiques, artwork, and delicate electronics, wooden packing and custom crating is the gold standard. Our craftspeople build custom wooden crates tailored to your item's exact dimensions, ensuring maximum protection during transport.

Each item is wrapped in protective materials, placed inside the custom crate, and sealed. This service is ideal for valuable possessions, overseas relocations, and moves across long distances where maximum protection is non-negotiable.

Custom wooden crates tailored to items
Premium protective wrapping and padding
Ideal for antiques, artwork, and electronics
